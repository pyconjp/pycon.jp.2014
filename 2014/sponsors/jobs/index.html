TriAxは、お客様が抱える「様々な課題」に立ち向かい解決に取り組む企業です。

大学・研究機関との強力なパートナーシップと、様々な企業へのプロジェクト参画を通して、 実用的な先端技術を追いかけていることがTriAxの強みです。

最高のアウトプットを出すために、最高の道具にこだわりたい。それが私たちが Python を選択する理由です。

Pythonistaの皆さん、新しい技術を知り実世界に応用することに興味を持っている方は、是非我々と一緒に働いてみませんか？ご連絡お待ちしております。

キャスタリア株式会社

http://www.castalia.co.jp/en/

FREEDOM is the word to describe the working style at Castalia, which reflects CEO Yamawaki’s intention to recreate the working style he experienced in New York, where he spent ten years. However, it does not take little for you to actually enjoy the FREEDOM; it is based on the premises that you are an autonomous worker, are not obsessed with certain places or time, and are capable of working as part of a group. In a way, as long as you can contribute work to us, we basically do not interfere with what you do – we respect to the utmost what you wish and aim to do as an individual.

[Job Description]
- Development of the “goocus” series, Castalia’s mobile learning platform

[Required Skills: one or more]
- Python
- iOS
- Android
- HTML5

[Additional Statement upon Hiring]
- We welcome those who prefer working silently and sedulously, who aim to develop their programming skills, and those who wish to make use of their English language skills to correspond with partners overseas.
- Educational background is not a factor for hiring.
- Feel free to choose your working place. Currently, our engineers work at our office in Aoyama, Tokyo, as well as from Nagano Prefecture and Hokkaido.
- Feel free to choose your contract style. While we prefer you to be our employee, we also welcome part-time and freelance workers.
- We are actively expanding projects overseas. Basically, all employees, regardless of their duties, are going on international business trips at least once a year. (The CEO pledged so at the start of this year.) In addition, we often have foreign staffs and you will be working in a flurry of English conversations.
